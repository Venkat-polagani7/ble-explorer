# Privacy Policy for BLE Explorer

**Effective Date:** April 30, 2026

BLE Explorer ("the App") is a professional Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) diagnostic tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection & Sharing
*   **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
*   **No Tracking:** The App does not use any analytics, advertising SDKs, or user tracking mechanisms.
*   **No Third-Party Sharing:** Since we do not collect any data, no data is ever shared with third parties.

## 2. Permissions & Usage
The App requests the following permissions solely to enable Bluetooth functionality as required by the Android and iOS operating systems:

*   **BLUETOOTH_SCAN & BLUETOOTH_CONNECT:** Required to discover, connect to, and communicate with nearby Bluetooth devices.
*   **BLUETOOTH_ADVERTISE:** Required only when using the "GATT Advertiser" feature to broadcast signals.
*   **ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION:** On older versions of Android (11 and below), the operating system requires location access to perform Bluetooth scans. **We do not record or use your GPS location.**
*   **INTERNET:** Used only if you explicitly choose to use a feature that requires it (e.g., viewing external developer documentation or DFU firmware links).

All Bluetooth operations happen entirely on your local device.

## 3. Data Storage
*   **Local Only:** All data generated during your session (scan results, device logs, diagnostic reports) is stored in the App's temporary memory or local storage.
*   **User-Initiated Export:** If you choose to export a Diagnostic Report (Excel), the file is saved to your device's local storage or shared via your system's share sheet. We never upload these files to our servers.

## 4. Security
We follow industry-standard best practices to ensure the App is secure. Because the App does not have a backend server or account system, your data is as secure as your mobile device itself.

## 5. Children's Privacy
BLE Explorer is a developer tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
